MSIS 547

Managing In The Era Of Cloud Computing

Course Overview

In the dynamic landscape of technology, Generative AI has emerged as a groundbreaking force, especially in the era of cloud computing. This course is designed to immerse students in the world of Generative AI, offering a comprehensive exploration from foundational theories to cutting-edge real-world applications. Each session blends interactive lectures, group discussions, hands-on coding tutorials, and live exercises, ensuring an engaging and participatory learning environment. We start with understanding AI's impact on work and creativity, diving into neural networks and deep learning. Progressing through computer vision and transfer learning, students will engage in practical exercises like image classification and text-to-image generation using diffusion models. The journey continues with a look at Natural Language Processing, attention mechanisms, and Transformers, culminating in hands-on experiences with large language models like ChatGPT and exploring the realms of multimodality and foundation models in cloud computing. The course not only focuses on technical skills but also emphasizes critical thinking, ethical considerations, and real-world problem-solving in the AI domain. A special guest lecture and group presentations will enrich this learning experience, preparing students for advanced academic pursuits or careers in this ever-evolving field.
